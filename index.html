About Us

Dhyanalinga Power Solutions had its beginning at 2014 with committed expertise in Coimbatore, Tamilnadu, India. We adopt best practices in the industry across all its support functions and focused approach towards building individual excellence and promoting customer satisfaction.

DHYANALINGA POWER SOLUTIONS is the authorised business partner for various leading power equipment manufacturers. Dhyanalinga Power Solutions comes with more than a decade of field experience and a team of experts in power electronics and power systems owing to its strong background in UPS and power conditioning/quality systems.

We stay close to our customers and cater to their needs through continuous technological innovation, rich expertise and customer centric approach, breadth of solutions, and a wide range of services to provide you uninterrupted power solutions, maximizing your infrastructure uptime. Our greatest strength is our ability to understand customer's unique requirements and offer effective & efficient solutions and services. We offer cost effective and value added specialty in power solution to all our customers.
